### NIH R01-MH137578 (NEW)
**Title:** Cellular and developmental genetic regulation of 3' isoform diversity in the human brain and its contribution to neuropsychiatric disorders
**Agency:** NIMH
**Period:** Sep 2024 - Jun 2029
**Role:** Contact PI (MPI with Ophir Shalem)
**FY2025 Funding:** $686,787
**Focus:** Alternative polyadenylation (APA) regulation in brain development and psychiatric disorders
**Aims:**
1. Generate comprehensive atlas of APA regulation across neurodevelopment (3650+ bulk samples, 700+ donors single-nucleus)
2. Train deep learning model predicting APA changes from sequence; integrate with psychiatric GWAS
3. Experimental validation via high-throughput screens and genome-engineering in iPSC-derived neurons

### SFARI Targeted #957585 (2022)
**Title:** Topology of Cortical and Thalamic Gene Expression Changes in Autism
**Agency:** Simons Foundation (Autism BrainNet Analysis)
**Role:** MPI (with Daniel Geschwind, Dorothy Schafer)
**Focus:** Thalamo-cortical circuit dysregulation in ASD using multi-omics
**Methods:** snMulti-omics (snRNA-seq + ATAC-seq), IsoSeq long-read, MERFISH spatial transcriptomics
**Samples:** 30 ASD + 30 controls from Autism BrainNet (cortex + thalamus)
**Aims:**
1. Characterize transcriptomic changes within connected thalamo-cortical circuits
2. Identify transcription factor regulatory networks for cell-type-specific disease signatures
3. Map disease-associated isoform expression changes
4. Determine spatial topology linking activated glia with disrupted neuronal signatures
